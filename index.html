Ironman is a Marvel Comics superhero created by Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, Don Heck, and Jack Kirby. His alter ego, Tony Stark, is a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor who builds a powered exoskeleton to save his life and fights crime. Ironman is a founding member of the Avengers.

SPIDER MAN
MARVEL
Spider-Man, created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko, is a Marvel Comics superhero. His alter ego, Peter Parker, is a high school student who gains spider-like abilities after a radioactive spider bite. Known for his agility, web-slinging, and "spidey-sense," he fights crime while balancing personal struggles.

DEADPOOL
MARVEL
Deadpool, created by Fabian Nicieza and Rob Liefeld, is a Marvel Comics anti-hero known for his dark humor, regenerative healing, and breaking the fourth wall. His alter ego, Wade Wilson, is a former mercenary who undergoes an experimental procedure to cure his cancer, gaining superhuman abilities but severe disfigurement.

DOCTOR STRANGE
MARVEL
Doctor Strange, created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko, is a Marvel Comics superhero. His alter ego, Dr. Stephen Strange, is a former neurosurgeon who becomes the Sorcerer Supreme after a car accident ends his surgical career. He defends Earth against mystical and magical threats using powerful spells and artifacts.

CAPTAIN AMERICA
MARVEL
Captain America, created in 1941, is Steve Rogers, transformed into a super-soldier by the Super-Soldier Serum. With his iconic vibranium shield, he symbolizes justice and freedom. Revived in modern times, he joins the Avengers, fighting threats like Hydra and Thanos, embodying unwavering morals, leadership, and heroism.

THOR
MARVEL
Thor, the Norse god of thunder, is a Marvel superhero created by Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, and Jack Kirby in 1962. Wielding the enchanted hammer Mjolnir, Thor possesses immense strength and control over lightning. As the Asgardian prince, he defends both Earth and the Nine Realms, balancing his godly heritage with human compassion.

HULK
MARVEL
The Hulk, a Marvel Comics character, is the alter ego of Dr. Bruce Banner. After a gamma radiation accident, Banner transforms into the Hulk when angry. The Hulk possesses immense strength and durability, often battling both heroes and villains. His struggle embodies the conflict between rage and reason.

BLACK PANTHER
MARVEL
Black Panther, a Marvel Comics character, is the superhero alias of T'Challa, king of the fictional African nation of Wakanda. He possesses enhanced abilities from the Heart-Shaped Herb and wears a vibranium suit. As Black Panther, T'Challa is a skilled warrior, protector of his people, and a symbol of African heritage and innovation.
